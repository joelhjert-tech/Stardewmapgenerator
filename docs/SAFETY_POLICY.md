# Safety Policy

- Generation is prototype-only unless explicitly promoted later.
- Do not overwrite production maps.
- Do not modify source maps.
- Do not commit `mission_assets`.
- Do not commit unpacked basegame files.
- Do not place structural tiles from loose single-tile role lists.
- Use marker fallback when templates are missing.
- Only locked Joel-approved blocks can be used for core structures.
- Floor blocks remain unapproved unless explicitly approved later.
- Keep source assets local and outside Git.
